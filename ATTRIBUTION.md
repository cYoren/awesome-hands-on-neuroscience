## Atlases and Parcellations

### AAL (Automated Anatomical Labeling)

- **Citation**: Tzourio-Mazoyer, N., Landeau, B., Papathanassiou, D., et al. (2002). Automated anatomical labeling of activations in SPM using a macroscopic anatomical parcellation of the MNI MRI single-subject brain. *NeuroImage*, 15(1), 273-289.
- **License**: Free for academic use

### Brodmann Areas

- **Citation**: Brodmann, K. (1909). *Vergleichende Lokalisationslehre der Grosshirnrinde*. Leipzig: Johann Ambrosius Barth.
- **Note**: Historical atlas, public domain

### Schaefer 2018 Parcellations

- **Citation**: Schaefer, A., Kong, R., Gordon, E.M., et al. (2018). Local-global parcellation of the human cerebral cortex from intrinsic functional connectivity MRI. *Cerebral Cortex*, 28(9), 3095-3114.
- **License**: [MIT](https://opensource.org/licenses/MIT)
- **Source**: https://github.com/ThomasYeoLab/CBIG

### Yeo 2011 Networks

- **Citation**: Yeo, B.T., Krienen, F.M., Sepulcre, J., et al. (2011). The organization of the human cerebral cortex estimated by intrinsic functional connectivity. *Journal of Neurophysiology*, 106(3), 1125-1165.
- **License**: Free for academic use

### MNI152 Template

- **Citation**: Fonov, V., Evans, A.C., Botteron, K., et al. (2011). Unbiased average age-appropriate atlases for pediatric studies. *NeuroImage*, 54(1), 313-327.
- **License**: Free for academic use
- **Source**: Montreal Neurological Institute

### Destrieux 2009 Atlas

- **Citation**: Destrieux, C., Fischl, B., Dale, A., & Halgren, E. (2010). Automatic parcellation of human cortical gyri and sulci using standard anatomical nomenclature. *NeuroImage*, 53(1), 1-15.
- **License**: Part of FreeSurfer distribution

### MSDL Atlas

- **Citation**: Varoquaux, G., Gramfort, A., Pedregosa, F., Michel, V., & Thirion, B. (2011). Multi-subject dictionary learning to segment an atlas of brain spontaneous activity. *Information Processing in Medical Imaging*, 562-573.
- **License**: Part of Nilearn distribution

### Smith 2009 RSN Maps

- **Citation**: Smith, S.M., Fox, P.T., Miller, K.L., et al. (2009). Correspondence of the brain's functional architecture during activation and rest. *Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences*, 106(31), 13040-13045.
- **License**: Free for academic use
